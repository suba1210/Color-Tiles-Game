Color Tiles - How To Play

  1. Hello Player, at the home page select the mode you want to play.
  2. Then, in each mode there's a small grid of colors, have a look at it.
  3. Then, Use all your Intellect to match the pattern of small grid inside the dark portion of the movable Color Tiles.
  4. If you won, your score will be displayed on the screen.
  5. Your Score is based on the Number of moves you did and the time you have taken to match the pattern.
  6. If you failed to match the pattern within the time, You will be lost, and your game will be stopped, then you can play again by clicking the "Play Again" button.
  7. The Highest Score you have scored so far is displayed on each modes of the game.
  8. Happy Gaming!
